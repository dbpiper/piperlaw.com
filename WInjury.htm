If you have been injured in an accident that arose in the course and scope of your employment in Tennessee, the Workers' Compensation Law of the State of Tennessee provides you with certain rights and remedies. You rights and remedies are defined by statutory law as interpreted by judge-made law. YOU NEED A TRIAL LAWYER ON YOUR SIDE. Remember, the insurance company representatives DO NOT  have your interests as their primary concern. You need a lawyer on your side to make certain that you get good medical care and fair compensation.

Work-related accidents often cause the injured worker and the injured worker's dependants to experience recuded income and earning capacity. The negative effects from a work-related injury often last a lifetime.
