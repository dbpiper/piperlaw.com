WORKERS' COMPENSATION REPRESENTATION
 
If you have been injured while at work, you and your dependents will probably experience the reduced income and earning capacity which results from such work-related accidents. You need a lawyer on your side to make sure that you get the compensation that Tennessee law dictates that you receive.
 
BANKRUPTCY COURT REPRESENTATION
 
If you have experienced financial difficulty, you may need the protection of the United States Bankruptcy Court in order to eliminate debts which you are unable to pay and/or to reorganize your financial situation and begin a debt repayment plan. You should also consider non-bankruptcy alternatives such as Consumer Credit Counseling. You can STOP foreclosures, judgments, garnishments, repossessions in many circumstances.
 
CRIMINAL DEFENSE REPRESENTATION
 
If you have been charged with a crime, you need the services of an experienced criminal defense advocate to represent your interests. David Piper has successfully represented Tennessee clients charged with a variety of crimes, including reckless endangerment, theft offenses such as aggravated burglary, and vehicular homicide. Put this experience to work for you.
