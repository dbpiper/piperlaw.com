Our Mission

We believe that the legal profession should provide cost-effective solutions to the legal problems of individuals. There are many lawyers that serve the insurance companies and other powerful business interests. However, David Piper's practice is limited to representing individuals.

LAW OFFICE PROFILE

David Piper is a 1989 graduate of the University of Tennessee College of Law. In 1989, David Piper completed his legal education at the University of Tennessee in 1989--earning a J.D. (Doctor of Jurisprudence) degree--and gained admission to the bar of the State of Tennessee. Prior to entering the J.D. program at the University of Tennessee (Knoxville), David attended Tennessee Technological University in Cookeville, Tennessee, where he earned a Bachelor of Science degree (B.S.--Psychology) in 1986.

David Piper opened a small law practice in Nashville, Tennessee in 1989.  David Piper continued in solo practice until 1996, when he and several other attorneys formed the law firm of Piper, Pulley & Fields, LLC. David Piper and his family decided to move home to Cannon County, Tennessee following the death of Dr. William A. Bryant (David's father-in-law) in 1997 and the Law Office of David Piper, P.C. is now located on the family farm in Woodbury, Tennessee.

David Piper is a member of the Tennessee Trial Lawyers Association (TTLA), the American Trial Lawyers Association (ATLA), and various other professional associations devoted to advancing the causes of civil justice.
